Personal income tax in Belgium has progressive tax rates. This means that the higher your income, the higher the tax rate you pay.

The progressive tax rates for income tax year 2024 (assessment year 2025) are:
25 % - from EUR 0.01 to EUR 15 820
40 % - from EUR 15 820 to EUR 27 920
45 % - from EUR 27 920 to EUR 48 320
50 % - more than EUR 48 320.
