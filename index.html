Personal income tax has progressive tax rates. This means that the higher your income is, the higher the rate of tax you pay.

The progressive tax rates for 2022 income are:
25 % - from EUR 0.01 to EUR 13 540
40 % - from EUR 13 540 to EUR 23 900
45 % - from EUR 23 900 to EUR 41 360
50 % - more than EUR 41 360.
